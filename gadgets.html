Alienware M14x


Good things always come in small package.
Be it Sunil Gavaskar or Sachin Tendulkar. This beast too can claim to be of the same league. Just 14' in size, this machine is a powerhouse. Aesthetically build with matt finish inside runs a core i7 Intel Sandybridge processor. Nvidia Optimus technology firepower running the intense graphic application makes this Alienware M14x the gaming rig I was so fondly waiting to have under my armor of gadgets.

Samsung Galaxy Note:


Its not a phone...its not a tablet...its a Note
Well that's how Samsung describes their new flagship and its so true. It meets my needs for both a smartphone and tablet and contrary to many reports fits in my pocket as well (Seriously it fits so perfectly) Samsung's innovative technology can be sensed here with the S-pen (a smart stylus that ships with this device). Though I feel a lot can be done with this S-pen its potential is still to be unearthed. Syncs with all my Google applications seamlessly and with AT&T's 4G LTE connection I have pretty good internet availability on the go.
